Our Story

Our awareness of good skincare has developed over the years. Testing proprietary solutions, refined by specialised research, we realised that the secret to healthy, glowing skin lies in finding the balance in our bodies and then supporting it with a few essential skincare steps based on advanced ingredients that work in concert with the skin's regenerative processes. As individuals with strong tendencies towards allergies and skin imperfections, we believe in the necessity of a holistic approach in order to achieve visible results.

Driven by a mission to elevate skincare to new heights, champion diversity, and instill confidence, we founded BOGDAO. As a family-owned business, our signature formulas reflecting our unwavering commitment to excellence. Join us on this transformative journey and let our products become an essential part of your daily ritual.

We use advanced plant-based ingredients from around the world to create a synergistic effect in skincare. Our products are versatile and can be used for different skin types, seasons and lifestyles. Our formulations contain cold-pressed oils rich in essential fatty acids, crystal clear waters with skin-soothing properties and effective bioactive ingredients extracted using advanced biotechnology methods from plants.
